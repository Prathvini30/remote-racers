WELCOME TO REMOTE RACERS

REMOTE RACERS is a place where passion meets precision! Located in the heart of Manipal county road, just off the Hosur road, we offer a top-tier experience for RC Cars enthusiasts of all levels, from curious beginners to seasoned pros. Our state-of-the-art facility features three difference track, RC DRIFT, RC DIRT AND RC SPEED allowing racers to try various driving skills. At REMOTE RACERS, we are driven by our love for the sport and our commitment to providing an unmatched Racing experience. Whether you're perfecting your technique or just getting started, we’ve built the best environment for you to feel the thrill of the RC cars Racing!

Our Goal

At REMOTE RACERS, BENGALURU we make it easy for everyone to get a taste of the action—whether you’re an experienced drifter or brand new to the scene. Don’t have your own car yet? No problem! Come down and try out one of our upper tier hire cars, expertly tuned to give you an authentic driving experience in Drift, Dirt and Speed tracks. These cars are fast, responsive, and perfect for anyone looking to test their skills before committing to purchasing their own setup.It’s a great, low-cost option to dive into the sport without the upfront investment, and our team is always on hand to offer tips and guidance to make sure you get the most out of your session. Whether you’re just curious or seriously considering getting into RC Cars driving, our hire cars are the perfect way to see what it’s all about. Give it a try and see if you catch the Race bug—we bet you will!
