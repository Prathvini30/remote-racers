WELCOME TO REMOTE RACERS

REMOTE RACERS is a place where passion meets precision! Located in the heart of Manipal county road, just off the Hosur road, we offer a top-tier experience for RC Cars enthusiasts of all levels. Our state-of-the-art facility features three different tracks—RC DRIFT, RC DIRT, and RC SPEED—allowing racers to hone various driving skills. Whether you're perfecting your technique or just getting started, we’ve built the best environment for you to feel the thrill of RC car racing!

Our Goal

At REMOTE RACERS, BENGALURU we make it easy for everyone to get a taste of the action—whether you’re an experienced drifter or brand new to the scene. Don’t have your own car yet? No problem! Try out one of our top-tier hire cars, expertly tuned for an authentic experience across Drift, Dirt, and Speed tracks. Our team is always on hand to offer tips and guidance so you get the most out of your session.
