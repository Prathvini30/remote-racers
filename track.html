OUR TRACK

Experience the thrill of RC DRIFTING

Get ready to drift on our purpose-built indoor circuit designed specifically for 1/14,16,18 scale RWD drift cars. Our smooth, low-grip surface allows for controlled slides and precise transitions, while the flowing layout features a mix of sweeping curves, tight hairpins, and technical switchbacks.

Whether you’re a seasoned pro perfecting your tandem lines or a newcomer just getting the feel for rear-wheel-drive drifting, this track provides the perfect environment to sharpen your skills. With ample run-off space and clearly marked zones, drivers can push their limits and enjoy the excitement of RC drifting in a safe, welcoming setting.

Come experience the unique challenges and adrenaline-fueled fun of our drift course and see why it’s a favourite destination for RC enthusiasts of all levels.
